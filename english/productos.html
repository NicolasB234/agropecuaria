Our products

The main products produced and exported by the group are: Tomatoes (Round, Pear, and Cherry), Peppers (Red, Green, and Yellow), Cassava, Zucchinis, Lemon (4 seasons), Eggplant, Strawberry (the area with the highest production in Corrientes), among others. We commercialize and export in bulk, packaged in plastic crates, wood crates, and cardboard boxes with the group's own brand of each company that comprises the group and very soon with a single brand owned by the exporting group and also with the possibility of the importer's brand. Palletized on pallets duly authorized for exportation with a maximum load capacity per pallet of 1500 kg each pallet. Depending on the product, each pallet can contain between 50 and 60 packages of a product.
