We Are What You Need

Always offering you balance and quality

We are a group of agricultural cooperatives in Corrientes, Bella Vista, exporting fresh products such as sweet potatoes, cassavas, bell peppers, strawberries, and more.

The group has a foreign trade area with qualified and specialized personnel in the FRUIT AND VEGETABLE SECTOR with over 4 years of experience advising companies and conducting market studies, market analysis, export plans for countries such as Brazil, Paraguay, Uruguay.

We are a group of 250 local producers in the fruit and vegetable sector in our region.

This group was formed in June 2023 thanks to the program of the Argentine Agency of Investments and International Trade (AAICI) and the ICBC Foundation.

Cooperativa Agropecuaria Progreso ltda.

The Cooperativa Agropecuaria Progreso Ltd. is located in Colonia Progreso, near Bella Vista, Corrientes, Argentina, and is dedicated to the production and commercialization of tomatoes, peppers, and citrus fruits. Dedicated to the production and commercialization of agricultural products, with 20 years in the regional and national market. It has 38 active members and has an estimated monthly production of 25 tons.

Cooperativa Agropecuaria La Estrella Ltda.

The Cooperativa Agropecuaria La Estrella Ltda. is located in Cruz de los Milagros, Corrientes, Argentina. It is dedicated to the production and commercialization of tomatoes, peppers, beans, jalapeño peppers, strawberries, sweet potatoes, corn, and cassava. It has 15-20 associates and has had sales volume of $5,000,000 in the last 3 years. Its production capacity is currently 4 tons per month.

Cooperativa Agropecuaria Juventud Unida Ltda.

The Cooperativa Agropecuaria Juventud Unida Ltda. was founded in 2002 in Colonia Tatacua, Concepción, Corrientes. It has around 20 producing members and stands out for its variety of production, including sweet potatoes, cassava, tomatoes, bell peppers, oranges, honey, pumpkins, etc. It currently has approximately 20 hectares of production and plans to expand in the future.

Cooperativa Agropecuaria Salvador Dana Ltda.

The Cooperativa Agropecuaria Salvador Dana Ltda. was created in 2014, has 11 members, and also works with non-member producers, it has producers from the Lavalle department and Goya department, its production includes cherry tomatoes, round tomatoes, pear tomatoes, beans, zucchinis, zucchinis, and peppers. It sells approximately 500 tons of production annually, all of which is sold domestically.

APPPC Asociación Provincial de Pequeños Productores Correntinos.

The APPPC Asociación Provincial de Pequeños Productores Correntinos. was created more than 25 years ago. With extensive experience in the domestic market, located in the town of Tres de Abril, Corrientes. Today it has more than 150 small and medium fruit and citrus producers.
